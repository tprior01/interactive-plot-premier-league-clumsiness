Premier League Clumsiness

All data scraped from the premier league website. Data begins from the 07/08 season. Data up-to-date until the end of the 21/22 season. Hover over the markers to see more information about each player. To highlight a player click on their marker or enter their name into the search bar. To query a sub-set of players interact with the widgets on the left and with the legend. Hover over the markers to see more information about each player. Total Mistakes is defined as the sum of red cards, own goals, penalties conceded and errors which led to a goal.
