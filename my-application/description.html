Premier League Clumsiness

All data scraped from the premier league website and begins from the 07/08 season. Interact with the widgets on the left and with the legend to query a subset of players to plot. Select a player to highlight and see a breakdown of errors per season on the bar chart below the scatter plot. Hover over the circles to see more information about each player. Total Mistakes is defined as the sum of red cards, penalties conceded, own goals and errors which led to a goal.
